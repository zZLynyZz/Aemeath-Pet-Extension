# Aemeath Pet - Chrome Extension 🌸

Bring Aemeath (from Wuthering Waves) directly to your web browser! This interactive Chrome Extension summons a customizable army of cute pixel-art pets that roam, fly, and even perform synchronized dances across your screen while you surf the web.

## ✨ Features (Version 2.0)

* 🐾 **Interactive Companions:** Drag, drop, and click your pets to interact with them. Watch them roam freely, fly around, or take a nap on your active tabs.
* 🎛️ **Full Customization:** Use the popup control panel to instantly adjust the pet size (60px - 250px) and summon a massive army of up to **25 pets** at once.
* ✨ **Smart Formations:** Command your pets to gather into organized shapes! Select from 5 different modes:
    * 1 Line, 2 Lines, 3 Lines
    * 'A' Shape
    * Heart Shape
    * *(Bonus: Summoning 20+ pets will automatically create stunning double-formations!)*
* 💃 **Concert Mode:** Hit the **Dance All** button to watch your entire pet army perform a synchronized jumping routine.
* 🔄 **Multi-Tab Synchronization:** Powered by Chrome Storage API, your pets' positions and states are seamlessly synchronized in real-time across all your open tabs without lagging your browser.
* 🛑 **Stand & Free Modes:** Freeze them in place with *Stand Mode*, or hit the *Free Pets* panic button to break any formation and let them roam wildly.

## 🚀 Installation Guide

Since this extension is not yet published on the Chrome Web Store, you can install it manually using Developer Mode:

1. **Download the source code:** Clone this repository or download it as a `.zip` file and extract it to a folder on your computer.
2. **Open Extensions Page:** Open Google Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/` (or click the puzzle piece icon > Manage extensions).
3. **Enable Developer Mode:** Toggle the **"Developer mode"** switch in the top right corner of the page.
4. **Load the Extension:** Click the **"Load unpacked"** button in the top left corner.
5. **Select the Folder:** Choose the folder where you extracted the project files.
6. **Enjoy!** Pin the Aemeath Pet icon to your toolbar, click it to open the control panel, and click **"Turn On"**!

## 🎮 How to Use

* **Size & Quantity:** Slide to scale your pets and spawn up to 25 instances.
* **Formation Mode:** Select your desired shape from the dropdown menu and click **Summon Formation**. The pets will gracefully fly to their designated coordinates.
* **Dance All:** Trigger a 4-second synchronized dance animation.
* **Free Pets:** Release the pets from their formation or dance routine, allowing them to return to random movements.
* **Stand Mode:** Toggle this to prevent pets from wandering around automatically.
